The main problems in learning

Neither school nor university teaches us how to study the material properly. We prepare for exams and learn the tests. We practice solving tasks of the same type in order to pass the test better, but often in the end this does not give us real knowledge. Cramming quickly wears off and is not helpful.

Сonclusion: you also need to be able to learn, but for some reason, it is rarely taught. What to do about it?

Specific techniques and exercises help to change the approach to learning, make it effective and exciting.

Learning techniques

Five practices from Barbara Oakley

  • Two types of focus

    Deep knowledge arises by alternating between focused and scattered thinking.

  • Recall

    Remember what you learned — it will allow you to connect scattered pieces of memory.

  • Interleaving

    Learn several skills at the same time, they enrich each other.

  • Questions

    As you listen to the teacher, come up with a question that you really care about.

  • The illusion of competence

    Repeat it to yourself, write it down, tell a friend: you only seem to know the new topic.

For those who like to procrastinate

Barbara Oakley’s Story.

Ever since she was a child, Barbara was not good at math. She considered herself a complete humanitarian, and not the smartest. In the army she studied Russian in order to get a raise, and so successfully that she was promoted to commander. But in order to get promoted, she had to pass math. And then Barbara came up with her own approach to the exact sciences. It turned out that if you are «bad at something,» your hard-won knowledge is much stronger than that of those who can understand everything straight away.
